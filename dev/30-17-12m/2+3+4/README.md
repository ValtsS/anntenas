WARC 30 m / 17m / 12 tri bander with 2+3+4 elements.

Single cable, all band driven elements are directly driven.

Modeled EZNEC Pro 7 and NEC-5 calculation engine.

Overall:

| Band        | Gain dBi          | F/B  | SWR |
| ------------- |:-------------:| -----:|-----:|
| 30m      | 6.2 | 10.8 | < 1.09  |
| 17m      | 7.6      |   29.5 | < 1.13 |
| 12m | 8.8      |   16.6 | < 1.05 |
